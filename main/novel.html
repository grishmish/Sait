Before the Republic

    Before the Republic is a publishing era used byLucas Licensing to denote material that is set between the years 37,000 BBY and 25,000 BBY in the Star Wars universe. Before the Republic was the seventh in-universe publishing era to be established, the first since the creation of the original five publishing eras in 2000, and was created to classify the Dark Horse comic series Star Wars: Dawn of the Jedi. The era includes the three arcs of Dawn of the Jedi, the tie-in novel Dawn of the Jedi: Into the Void, and two accompanying short stories.

  • Dawn of the Jedi: Into the Void (2013)

The Old Republic

    The Old Republic era takes place from 25,053 BBY (the founding of the Republic), until 1000 BBY (the Seventh Battle of Ruusan and the end of the New Sith Wars).

  • Star Wars: The Old Republic: Revan (2011)
  • Star Wars:The Old Republic: Deceived (2011)
  • Star Wars: The Old Republic: Fatal Alliance (2010)
  • Star Wars: The Old Republic: Annihilation (2012)
